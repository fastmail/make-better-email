So you want to attend Make Better Email 2023? Great!

First off, this event is by invitation only. If you didn't get an invitation, and you think you should have gotten one, you should contact us and ask! Tell us what service or software you work on. (Remember, we're focusing on IMAP clients and servers, this time!)

If you have an invitation, it will say what you need to do:

  • register at the event registration page
  • book your travel and hotel for Philadelphia (be here October 30th through November 2nd!)
  • write in with things you'd like to get on the agenda

We'd like to keep things small. That means just one or two people per client or server. We're not planning on remote attendees by video conference right now. If you're invited but can't make it, we can keep you up to date over email or chat.
